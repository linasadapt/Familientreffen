Things we might do together.

  • A family quiz or Kahoot — easy, fun, multi-generational.
  • Photo sharing — bring a few favourites from your branch of the family.
  • Family history snippets — short stories that connect us.
  • Outdoor games and walks for those who want to move.
  • Kids-friendly activities and quiet corners for the small ones.
  • An optional Sunday walk in the Druskininkai area.
